# 📱 SmartExpense Tracker

A lightweight, intuitive personal finance application designed to help users track their daily income and expenses, manage budgets, and visualize spending habits.

---

## 🚀 Features

* **Real-time Expense Tracking:** Add, edit, or delete transactions instantly.
* **Categorization:** Organize your spending into custom categories (e.g., Food, Transport, Entertainment, Utilities).
* **Visual Analytics:** Clean charts and progress bars to view your monthly budget summary at a glance.
* **Secure Storage:** Keeps your data persistent and safe.
